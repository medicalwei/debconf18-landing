DebConf in Taiwan!

The 19th Debian Conference

In 2018, DebConf will be hosted in Hsinchu, Taiwan.

DebConf is the annual developers’ conference of the Debian project, one of the largest Free Software projects worldwide.

Debian is non-commercial and vendor-independent. DebConf allows developers from all over the world, who all volunteer their time, to gather once a year and concentrate on the development and improvement of the Debian distribution.
